Hi, I’m Hailee!

I’m an engineer turned researcher and designer based in Seattle, WA.

I love to serve as a bridge between people and technology. I believe that collaborative, human-centered practices can lead us to tools that can enrich our lives and communities.

I’m happiest when talking with people to better understand their authentic lived experiences and empowering them to have a voice in design solutions. All the while, my background in computer engineering and passion for technology helps keep me grounded in meaningful practical solutions.
